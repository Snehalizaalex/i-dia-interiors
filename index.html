PROFESSIONAL SUPPORT SERVICES

(For Designers, Carpenters & Architects)

We provide professional interior design support services for carpenters, freelance designers, architects, and contractors who require technical drawings, 3D visualization, production details, or site execution guidance for their projects.

Our team can support your project at any stage of development — from concept design to execution.

Our Support Services

2D Technical Drawings

Professional working drawings required for accurate furniture production and site execution.

Includes:

  • ✔ Furniture layout plans
  • ✔ Kitchen working drawings
  • ✔ Wardrobe working drawings
  • ✔ Section drawings
  • ✔ Detailed measurements

Software Used: AutoCAD / Cabinet Vision

3D Design & Visualization

High quality 3D interior design visualization to present projects professionally to clients.

Includes:

  • ✔ Photorealistic 3D views
  • ✔ Material & color selection
  • ✔ Lighting concepts
  • ✔ Multiple angles

Software Used: SketchUp / Vray / Lumion

Cutlist & Production Details

Accurate cutting list and production details for carpenters and modular furniture manufacturers.

Includes:

  • ✔ Panel cutting sizes
  • ✔ Material breakdown
  • ✔ Hardware placement
  • ✔ Edge band details

Software Used: Cabinet Vision

Site Execution Support

Professional assistance for interior execution and installation.

Includes:

  • ✔ Execution guidance
  • ✔ Design clarification
  • ✔ Installation support
  • ✔ Technical problem solving
